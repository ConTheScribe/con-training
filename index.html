Non-negotiable rules
1
Protein at every meal. Eggs, fish, chicken, cottage cheese, legumes. Every meal has a protein source. This is the single most important rule for the build you want.
2
2.5–3 litres of water daily. You're sweating a lot. Dehydration tanks performance, recovery, and cognitive function. Get a 1L bottle and fill it 3x.
3
Don't fear carbs. You're doing hard calisthenics. Rice, oats, sweet potato, banana — these fuel your sessions. The goal is lean, not no-carb.
4
Consistent meal timing matters more than perfect food choices. Eating at roughly the same times daily stabilises blood sugar, energy and mood — especially important on Lamotrigine.
5
The 80/20 principle. Eat clean 80% of the time and the 20% takes care of itself. One KFC, one soda, one indulgent meal — these don't derail anything if the baseline is solid.
